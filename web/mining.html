Level 0-12 Mine sand in the Mushroom Desert until mining 12. Get a golden shovel with eff 5. Then just buy 15 of the cheapest enchanted ore and give to rhys in the Deep Caverns. From now on skill levels only matter for small boosts and some reforges, focus mainly on your Heart of the Mountain level. Until Hotm 4 you should just do commissions while farming for gear. Get glacite armor and progress through the pickaxes from the shopkeeper(Bubu) next to the spawn. You should just buy glacite from the auction house to speed up commissions immediately, instead of grinding glacite walkers. You will need to grind them later anyway so you could if you want. For equipment, just forge the mithril set in the forge and using the dwarven treasures you get from events(always do events), reforge them to royal.
